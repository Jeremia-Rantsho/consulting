We Are

Mokoena and Mokoena Consulting; and as 100% black-owned and professionally run business. The decision to formally register the business was based on the decision by the owner to enter the mainstream of the economy, after having identified a gap in the market for the supply and delivery of products and services that were much needed by the public and private sector. We believe our business can offer our clients with products and services at a reasonable cost, superior quality and service.

It is envisaged Mokoena and Mokoena Consulting will grow from small operations, to be a well-managed and profitable company that strives to empower the previously disadvantaged communities through skills transfer, whilst providing reliable and efficient services to their customers and improve the company bottom line.

In addition, the company will strive to be a business of choice within the public and private sector. The business will thrive on the fact that it will create a rapport with its clients who often refer business to us. Currently the business is looking for avenues to grow its revenues and clientele.

Boipelo Goeieman. She is a poultry farmer,farming broilers based in the Northwest ,Taung from Madipelesa village in greater Taung Municipality. She started operating in 2020 during COVID. When she first started she had only the passion and the skill of running the business and handling money but she did not have clear idea of the financials until SEDA invited her to a financial management training that was facilitated by Mokoena and Mokoena Consulting. She made sure that she absord as much information she could from the training so that she can go back home and manage her business and personal finances better. "Mokoena mokoena facilitator made sure that they give us the tools to help us budget and manage our finances in a smart way, which is very crucial to know what you are doing when running a business. And to Mokoena and Mokoena company keep saving entrepreneurs businesses by giving them the right tools", she said
